Pup Home

Pup Home is a web application designed to help people find lost pets and rehome animals for adoption with love and care. Built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Firebase, it lets users post pet details, upload multiple images, search by location, and securely manage their listings with login-based access. The platform promotes responsible, non-commercial pet adoption and makes it easy to connect caring owners with those looking to adopt or help
